Codenvy Enterprise is a private cloud version of Codenvy, installed and managed behind your firewall.

A centrally-managed development environment solves many thorny issues related to IP drift, provisioning new projects, and IDE configuration.

Codenvy Enterprise provides mechanisms to independently scale the editing, build, and test VMs allocated to projects

Development teams connect through their browser and gain access to all of Codenvy’s features including Version Control System integration, PaaS bindings, editing, and real-time collaboration.
