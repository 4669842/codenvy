Marketing Program Manager

Position Overview

Codenvy is seeking to recruit the very best Marketing Program Manager. We need a highly energetic and innovative program manager who will work tirelessly to promote Codenvy and position us in a positive light. You must be an excellent communicator, have a love of software developers, and willing to take a "do anything to make it happen" attitude. You must be confident, organized, and adaptable.

This position will be responsible for promoting Codenvy through a number of channels and platforms. This includes overseeing the Web site, email marketing, event management, social media, advertising, and PR. We are a highly metric-driven organization, and the program manager will own the development, improvement, and achievement of a variety of critical user & customer activity, engagement, and loyalty KPIs.

Responsibilities

  1. Multi-channel program planning, execution and measurement.
  2. Case studies & customer engagement.
  3. Lead and manage direct marketing, PR, advertising, online marketing, event marketing and internal creative services.
  4. Help win early customers and accounts with a limited budget.

Job Requirements

  1. 1) Heart, commitment, and passion.
  2. Experience executing and organizing a variety of email, social, event, and advertising programs.
  3. A track record of performance excellence meeting targets and objectives.
  4. Excellent communication skills.
  5. Possess a customer service orientation.
  6. Ability to manage multiple, complex priorities within demanding timeframes.
  7. An incredible eye for detail, and a perfectionist attitude.
  8. Experience with any of the following will win major kudos: Marketo, PHPList, HootSuite, Google Analytics, Saleforce.com.
  9. Good with Excel graphs and metrics.
  10. Google Webmaster tools experience.
