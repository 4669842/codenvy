Codebase Development Environment

No one would ever rightly suggest that programmers use a desktop IDE to maintain the utmost levels of security and compliance when dealing with things like medical records or payment information. Codenvy is the best Codebase development environment because its compliance standards make it easy for developers to comply with information security standards and regulations.

Desktop IDEs vary wildly from one to another, but being able to achieve a high level of security necessary for governmental compliance standards is difficult for any one of them. There are such disparate approaches developer can take to ensuring the upkeep of their coding environments that it's a wonder they would even try to achieve compliance at all. There's simply no way to guarantee that a desktop IDE's security protocols will be able to uphold the high standards set by governmental regulation.

Codebase development environments like Codenvy come with an industry-leading ability to conform to strict security standards across an entire enterprise team without the bother of overseeing developer's individual coding environments. Codebase development environments, specifically Codenvy, can easily comply with information security standards and regulations, including Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX), and Payment Car Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS). These types of things make Codenvy, a Codebase development environment at the head of its class, an outstanding tool for enterprise teams to use in lieu of traditional solutions.
