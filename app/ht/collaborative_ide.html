Collaborative IDE

Many developers have expressed frustration over the difficulty and complexity of maintaining a development environment utilizing desktop IDEs. Codenvy is the best Collaborative IDE for developers to leave those frustrations behind.

Desktop IDEs are basically miniature data centers developers have to operate, which takes up valuable time and effort they could otherwise spend programming. A comprehensive code, test, and build environment usually requires the maintenance and upkeep of multiple components and software systems. A recent study has shown that developers can spend up to one-third of their time administering their desktop IDE.

Collaborative IDEs, on the other hand, give programmers a break from this work by handling most of these time-consuming tasks. Collaborative IDEs like Codenvy let developers spend less time administering their desktops and more time programming. Developers accomplish this by switching to a collaborative IDE that gives them greater functionality, flexibility and customization.
