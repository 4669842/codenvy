About us

The technical underpinnings of the IT world have matured: we have better browsers, faster networks, reliable cloud infrastructures and multi-core processors. At Codenvy, we embrace these changes to find new solutions to old problems. Our cloud environment for coding, building, and testing apps uses cloud's infinite resources to shorten development tasks while organizations simplify on-boarding, collaboration and compliance processes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize technical workbenches by leveraging cloud’s collaborative, shared, and elastic attributes to make developers more productive. We strive for simplicity, efficiency and productivity.

Our Team

We love technology and are passionate about building great products. Our leadership team has backgrounds from RedHat, HP, BEA/Oracle, Quest, and Veritas. They’ve also worked on multiple start-ups in spaces directly relevant to cloud, middleware, and application development.

We are building a company that people love to work with, and we hope you will too.

There are 4 direct costs to managing a desktop IDE.

  • Installation
  • Maintenance
  • Merge
  • Starvation

As a cloud-based IDE, Codenvy eliminates many of the costs of maintaining a desktop development environment.

Codenvy knows Java syntax, formatting and language nuances. As a cloud IDE for Java, it supports frameworks from multiple Platform as a Service (PaaS) vendors, and we are adding more PaaS support continuously.

Codenvy is the cloud development environment for coding, building, and testing applications. Codenvy's 70,000 developers gain 12 hours of additional time each week by eliminating desktop reconfiguration. Codenvy cloud workspaces are automatically provisioned, browser-accessible, and shared as part of a global community. Codenvy Enterprise is commercially provided for on premise operation.

Codenvy has the largest selection of integrated partners including GitHub, BitBucket, RedHat OpenShift, Google App Engine, Amazon Web Services BeanStalk, VMware CloudFoundry, Heroku, AppFog, CloudBees, Tier3, and ZeroTurnaround.
