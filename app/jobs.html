Graphic Designer

We have a unique opportunity for a talented graphic designer to help us craft the future of our service. Based in San Francisco, you will lead design efforts for our product and brand. You will work closely with our CEO, Product Managers and R&D teams to help us build a beautiful end-product. Additionally, you will have the opportunity to make high-level contributions to product strategy and vision.

You should have phenomenal taste, meticulous attention to detail and the desire to help us build and grow the company.

You should be proficient with Adobe Creative Suite and have basic knowledge of HTML/CSS. Some additional development experience is a plus.

What is in this for you?

This will be a once in a lifetime experience. Here is what we can offer:

  • Competitive salary and equity package.
  • The opportunity to establish your mark by becoming the brand and design leader of a high potential product.
  • The pleasure to work with an awesome team of nice and smart people.
