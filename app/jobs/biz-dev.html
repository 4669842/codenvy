Director, Business Development

Position Overview

The Director, Business Development will be Codenvy's first revenue accountable hire. You will be the force that transforms us from leading technology provider to validated and growing business. This positions will create our revenue acquisition strategy in a manner that supports consistent business growth and enhances brand equity and awareness. This includes identifying potential deals and bringing them to fruition.

Responsibilities

  1. Develop unique value propositions, business partnerships, category management, as well as call to action oriented advertising targeted toward key customer segments.
  2. Drive business development, evangelism, and partner engagement, with targeted adoption of APIs, extension architecture along with content creation supporting it's actionable adoption.
  3. Manage multiple business initiatives in a startup environment.
  4. Manage complex negotiation and work with legal counsel as required.
  5. Win early customers and partners with limited support & resources.

Job Requirements

  1. A track record of excellence.
  2. A love of technology and developers.
  3. An ability to thrive with the pressures of being revenue accountable.
