About us

The technical underpinnings of the IT world have matured: we have better browsers, faster networks, reliable cloud infrastructures and multi-core processors. At Codenvy, we embrace these changes to find new solutions to old problems. Our cloud environment for coding, building, and testing apps uses cloud's infinite resources to shorten development tasks while organizations simplify on-boarding, collaboration and compliance processes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize technical workbenches by leveraging cloud’s collaborative, shared, and elastic attributes to make developers more productive. We strive for simplicity, efficiency and productivity.

Our Team

We love technology and are passionate about building great products. Our leadership team has backgrounds from RedHat, HP, BEA/Oracle, Quest, and Veritas. They’ve also worked on multiple start-ups in spaces directly relevant to cloud, middleware, and application development.

We are building a company that people love to work with, and we hope you will too.

Code, build, and test in the cloud for free at http://codenvy.com.

Codenvy is the cloud development environment for coding, building, and testing applications. Codenvy's 50,000 developers gain up to two additional hours of viable coding time each day due to cloud-powered always-on workspaces, parallel multi-core compilation, continuous iterative deployment, and multi-cursor pair programming. For more information visit http://codenvy.com.

Codenvy users come from the world's best companies including NBC, Stanford, Cisco, eBay, Paypal, VMware, Intel, Nasa, BMW, Google, American Express, Amazon, and GE.

We have offices located in San Francisco and Ukraine and have raised $9M from Toba Capital, Auriga Ventures, and a variety of angels.
