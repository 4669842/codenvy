Vice President, Marketing

Position Overview

Reporting to the CEO, the Vice President of Marketing & Business Development is responsible for developing and executing a clearly defined marketing, communications, and business development strategy in a manner that supports consistent business growth and enhances brand equity and awareness. This position is responsible for planning, organizing, staffing, training, and managing all marketing functions to achieve the Company objectives for sales, growth, profitability, and visibility while ensuring a consistent marketing message and position consistent with the corporate direction. Additionally, this start up requires this executive to drive business development which will identify potential deals and develop tactics needed to bring them to fruition.

Responsibilities

  1. Create, manage, coordinate and execute all marketing strategies and implement tactical plans that meet and exceed company expectations.
  2. Develop unique value propositions, business partnerships, category management, as well as call to action oriented advertising targeted toward key customer segments
  3. Lead and manage direct marketing, PR, advertising, online marketing, event marketing and internal creative services.
  4. Oversee creative resources that engage into various product lines, subsequently extending our brand identity into our offerings.
  5. Achieve targeted results and ensure timely and effective execution of marketing tactics and programs.
  6. Analyze, evaluate, plan and execute on both existing and potential marketing activities and strategies. Develop benchmark criteria to measure the efficiency and effectiveness of marketing programs.
  7. Conduct and analyze market research to determine competitiveness.
  8. Support the creation of an analytics driven business, where all decisions are defined and sourced through data.
  9. Identify, interpret, and capitalize on marketing trends.
  10. Manage the marketing budget.
  11. Develop and prepare key messaging and presentation material for senior management.
  12. Develop and execute marketing strategies related to community, engagement, stickiness, and loyalty related to targeted user and buying groups.
  13. Drive business development, evangelism, and partner engagement, with targeted adoption of APIs, extension architecture along with content creation supporting it's actionable adoption.
  14. Manage multiple business initiatives in a startup environment.
  15. Build and manage a technical evangelism team.
  16. Manage complex negotiation and work with legal counsel as required.
  17. Win early customers with limited support & resources.

Job Requirements

  1. 15 years with 7 years in progressive marketing roles at the senior management level.
  2. University degree or college diploma in business, MBA preferred.
  3. A track record of performance excellence meeting targets and objectives.
  4. Strong analytical, planning, forecasting and budgeting / financial skills.
  5. Excellent communication and negotiation skills.
  6. Experience with business development and M&A transactions.
  7. Minimum 5 years of experience in software development & ISV markets, with a strong preference for direct developer relations.
  8. Possess a customer service orientation.
  9. Ability to articulate a vision and strategy in a way that inspires and motivates a team.
  10. Ability to manage multiple, complex priorities within demanding timeframes.
